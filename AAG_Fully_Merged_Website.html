Tax

ITR Filing

We file ITR-1 to ITR-7 with complete support for salaried individuals, investors, freelancers, business owners, professionals, firms, LLPs, companies, trusts, and other eligible taxpayers.

  • ITR form selection and document review
  • ITR-1 to ITR-7 filing for all eligible cases
  • Refund follow-up and notice support
Salaried Freelancers Business Owners Companies
ITR-1

Best for simple salary-based returns with limited other income.

ITR-2

For individuals and HUFs with capital gains, house property, or foreign assets.

ITR-3 / 4

For business, professional, and presumptive income cases.

ITR-5 / 6 / 7

For firms, LLPs, companies, trusts, and similar entities.

Why clients choose us for ITR filing

We simplify the filing process, identify the correct return form early, review income and deductions carefully, and help reduce mistakes that can delay refunds or cause avoidable notices.
